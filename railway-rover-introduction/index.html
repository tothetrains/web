Railway Rover: Introduction

2 September 2021 by Seb J.

Introducing Railway Rover, your all line rover ticket to the latest in the rail industry. Be sure to check back in a few weeks for the first episode from Seb from To The Trains and a special guest from the community. They'll be discussing everything from rolling stock to the routes of the UK's various train operating companies.

Railway Rover

Railway Rover is your all line rover ticket to the latest in the rail industry. It's a podcast covering everything from rolling stock to routes, laid-back and conversational by design, but packed with plenty of information in a long format. You can:

  • Find all episodes on tothetrains.uk
  • Listen on Spotify
  • Listen on Apple Podcasts
  • Listen on Google Podcasts

You can also listen on all other major podcast listening platforms. Just search for "Railway Rover" to get started.
